Principal Limit Factor (PLF) Calculator

The PLF determines what percentage of your home's value (or FHA limit) you can access through a HECM reverse mortgage. It's based on the youngest borrower's age and the expected interest rate.

Calculate Your PLF

At age 70 with a 7.00% expected rate, you can access approximately 46.5% of your home's value.

Age Matters

Older borrowers qualify for higher PLFs. Each year of age increases the percentage you can access.

Rates Impact

Lower expected interest rates result in higher PLFs. Shop for the best rate to maximize proceeds.

Updated Regularly

HUD updates PLF tables periodically. These values are approximate - always verify with official sources.
